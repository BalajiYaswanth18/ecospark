Our Team

Welcome to ECO-SPARK! We are a passionate team of students from CMR College of Engineering and Technology, Hyderabad, currently in our 2nd year of BTech. Committed to making a positive impact on the environment, we have come together to create a platform that promotes responsible e-waste management.

Our goal is to raise awareness about the environmental and health effects of improper e-waste disposal. Through this platform, we aim to simplify the process of locating the nearest e-waste facilities and encourage responsible disposal practices. Join us on this journey towards a greener and cleaner future!
